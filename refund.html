Return & Refund Policy

Simple • Honest • Transparent

At Sundus Book Store, every book is packed with care to ensure it reaches you in perfect condition.

Return Policy

Returns are accepted only if:

  • The book is damaged
  • You received the wrong book

You must contact us within 48 hours of delivery for return approval.

Refund Policy

Once your return is approved, refunds will be processed within 5–7 business days.

Cash on Delivery

COD orders are not refundable once delivered, unless the product is damaged.
